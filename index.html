About Company
Company Overview

Lenovo PCCW Solutions supports organisations across different industries to accelerate digitalisation, boost operational efficiencies and reduce cost by tailoring IT solutions to meet their business needs.

Lenovo PCCW Solutions is a leading technology solutions powerhouse in Asia Pacific. A strategic partnership formed between Lenovo and PCCW in August 2022, the company offers market-leading solutions to help organizations transform their operations, increase competitiveness, and enhance innovation through technology.

Its wide range of technology solutions and service offerings include system integration, application development and operation, IT outsourcing, managed services, digital solutions, and technical services.

Whether serving its customers, collaborating with partners, working with people or contributing to the community, Lenovo PCCW Solutions believes in nurturing long-term trusted relationships and building a widespread network of collaboration.
